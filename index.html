Protect your test assets by certifying your staff

Companies like you spend hundreds of thousands of dollars on test fixtures but lack the staff to keep them production ready. Get your staff trained and increase your yields, reduce your downtime and eliminate emergency repairs.

Who we are

Joule University emerged from the realization that in an industry as complex as test engineering, employee training is often looked over. Joule University is focused on bringing you to a high level of internal training competency. This will allow you to direct your full attention to customer satisfaction and doing what you do best: creating products that make an impact.
